## About

Google Adwords is an app for tracking the activities of visitors to your ecommerce. Tool used to evaluate the effectiveness of your ads.
To see all the releases, [access the changelog](https://github.com/vtex-apps/google-adwords/blob/master/CHANGELOG.md)

## Features 

- Integration of your VTEX store with Google Adwords.
- A simple way to analyze your audience.

## How to configure

The required configuration will be to add the Adwords ID into your account. In the Admin, go to **Apps -> Google Adwords** and add the ID in the available field.
Learn more about the app [on the Github README.](https://github.com/vtex-apps/google-adwords/blob/master/README.md)
